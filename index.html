MASTER CLASSES

Satellite Design

This master class offers a comprehensive learning experience in the design and development of CubeSats or nano-satellites. Participants will dive into satellite architecture, systems integration, and virtual testing, gaining valuable, hands-on experience in satellite technology. By the end of the course, participants will have the knowledge to design and simulate their own satellite. It’s an ideal opportunity for university students and early-career professionals to explore satellite engineering and telemetry in a practical setting.

Remote Sensing

Focused on real-world applications, this master class teaches participants how to use remote sensing technologies and satellite data to monitor environmental changes. From tracking deforestation to analyzing water resources, participants will learn data analysis techniques that are critical in environmental science today. By the end of the program, they will contribute to an environmental monitoring report using satellite data. This course is perfect for environmental scientists, remote sensing experts, and students eager to explore space technology’s role in sustainability.

Robotics

This master class focuses on the applications of robotics in the space industry, covering topics like autonomous systems, robotic exploration, and satellite servicing. Participants will gain hands-on experience with robotic systems and learn how automation is revolutionizing space missions and satellite operations. This course is ideal for those interested in both robotics and space technology, equipping them with the skills to contribute to future space missions.

Navigation

This master class covers the principles and applications of navigation and positioning systems both on Earth and in spacecraft. Participants will explore GPS technology, satellite-based navigation, geolocation techniques, and how these systems are used for precise positioning in space missions. From everyday navigation to advanced spacecraft guidance, this course offers comprehensive knowledge, ideal for those looking to understand the critical role of navigation in space and Earth-based technologies.

Rocket Science

This master class delves deeply into the core concepts of rocket science and spacecraft propulsion, offering an in-depth understanding of the fundamental principles that govern rocket technology. Participants will explore the physics of thrust, fuel efficiency, and aerodynamics while learning about cutting-edge technologies such as cryogenic engines, electric propulsion systems, and multi-stage rockets. Additionally, the course covers crucial aspects of rocket design, trajectory planning, and the development of reusable launch systems that are revolutionizing modern space exploration. With hands-on exercises, interactive simulations, and expert insights, this master class is perfect for aspiring aerospace engineers.

Astronomy

This master class provides an in-depth understanding of astronomy, from celestial mechanics to organizing educational outreach programs. Participants will learn how to develop astronomy teaching materials and host virtual stargazing events, making astronomy more accessible to the public and students. By the end, they will have created educational resources that can be used in schools or on online platforms. This course is ideal for educators, amateur astronomers, and science communicators who want to deepen their knowledge and engage others in the wonders of astronomy.
